### 📖 Overview 

Climate change is often discussed through reports, statistics, and headlines. But what does it actually look like over time?

**The Earth Kept a Record. Here it is.** is an interactive climate dashboard that transforms historical climate data into engaging visual stories. Users can explore both **Cities Mode** and **Countries Mode**, compare warming trends, discover historical patterns, and visualise future predictions.

This project makes decades of climate data understandable, interactive, and accessible to everyone.

___

## ✨ Features 

### 📈 Temperature Trends
Explore how temperatures have changed over time for individual cities and countries.

### 🌍 Compare Locations
Compare multiple cities or countries o the same graph

### 🏆 Warming Rankings 
Discover which locations have experienced the highest warming.

### 🔮 Future Predictions
Explore future temperature trends of the countries by changing the positive contributing factors and the negative contributing factors to climate change using predictive models.

### 🌎 Dual View System 
User's can switch according to their desire between:

- Cities Mode
- Countries Mode

And the objective for this is for them to obtain better understanding of climate change.
